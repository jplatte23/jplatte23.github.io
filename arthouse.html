Art House

Engineer | Unity, C#, Perforce

The Game

In Art House, step into the life of Lisa, mother to Ollie and Zoe, who's struggling to pick up the pieces of her life after the passing of her mother, Betty. After returning to Betty's mountain home, Lisa and her kids get trapped inside of Betty's paintings where her memories come to life. Guide Lisa through hauntingly beautiful paintings while solving puzzles and confronting the shadows of the family’s past. Navigate a journey through grief and towards healing that lingers after the final brushstroke. This was a year long Unity project made by a team of over 30 people including a 5 person engineering team I was part of.

My Contributions

  • Fixed 50+ bugs that we tracked through tasks on Linear
  • Prototyped and implemented obstacles including lightning clouds and rips (portals)
  • Continuously improved scripts on enemy behaviors to improve player experience especially on the shifters
  • Coordinated with the design and QA team to make sure we followed our design docs
