Art House

Engineer | Unity, C#, Perforce

The Game

Art House is a narrative platformer about grief and memory, where players explore hand-painted worlds and solve traversal puzzles that evolve across scenes. Built in Unity as a year-long capstone by a 30+ person team with a 5-engineer pod, with my work focused on gameplay systems, obstacle mechanics, and bug fixing during iteration and polish.

Technical Focus

  • Gameplay Systems: interactive obstacles, puzzle/traversal mechanics, enemy behavior scripting
  • Iteration + Bug Fixing: reproducing playtest issues, tracing root causes, validating fixes across scenes
  • Collaboration: designer-driven iteration and polish with clear gameplay goals and constraints
  • Tools: Unity, C#, Perforce, Linear
